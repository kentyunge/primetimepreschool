Enrollment

Children must be 3 years (born before September 1, 2017) for the T/Th class.  For the M/W/F a.m. and p.m. class, children must be 4 years old (born before September 1, 2016).

As required by our State license, all children must be toilet trained before attending Prime Time.

A $75 enrollment fee must accompany the application along with a copy of your child's birth certificate.  This will be refunded only if your child cannot be placed in any Prime Time class.
